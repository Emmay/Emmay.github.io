Bootstrap:Reactive

Bootstrap:Reactive is a Computer Science curricular module for 8th-12th grade students which teaches data structures, event-driven programming, animation, and software testing.

From July 2015 onwards, I re-wrote and translated the Bootstrap:Reactive lessons, activities, and software libraries, and created professional development workshops delivered at CSPdWeek in Golden, CO Summer 2016 and 2017.
